The ocean, covering over 70% of Earth's surface, is a realm of beauty, mystery, and unparalleled diversity. Beneath its waves lies a world teeming with life, from the smallest plankton to majestic whales. The ocean not only shapes our climate but also provides food, oxygen, and a home to millions of species.

Quick facts about the Ocean

  • Covers 71% of Earths surface

  • Home to 230,000 known species (and counting)

  • Produces over 50% of the worlds oxygen.

  • Contains 97% of the Earths water.

  • The deepest point humans have ever explored would be the Mariana Trench, at a whopping 10,994 meters
